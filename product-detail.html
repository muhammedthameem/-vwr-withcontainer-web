Description

Wheaton® CompletePAK® is a range of primary packaging components that are stocked ready-to-use and are customizable for most drug applications

  • Off-the-shelf, ready-to-use products
  • One source for total packaging solutions
  • Variety of different sterile components; a kit for any application
  • Complete manufacturing traceability

Each kit component is particulate cleaned, depyrogenated, and sterilized, arriving in the lab ready-to-use. Vials are packed in a sterile polypropylene tray which can be used as a filling aid. Kit packaging is sized to compliment manual aseptic filling processes.

The CompletePAK® “single source” focus can reduce customer’s supply chain and time to market. Vials, stoppers, and seals are particulate cleaned, depyrogenated and sterilized, arriving in the lab ready-to-use.
